How to Fix Slow WiFi Speed

If your WiFi is slow, try these solutions to improve speed.

1. Restart Router

Turn off your router for 30 seconds and turn it back on.

2. Move Closer to Router

Distance affects signal strength.

3. Limit Connected Devices

Too many users reduce speed.

4. Change WiFi Channel

Switch to a less crowded channel.

5. Update Router Firmware

Keep your router updated for better performance.

Conclusion: These steps can significantly improve your WiFi speed.
