How to Fix Mic Not Working in PC

Problem: Your microphone is not working on your computer.

If your mic is not working, follow these simple steps:

Step 1: Check Microphone Settings

Go to sound settings and make sure your mic is selected.

Step 2: Allow App Permissions

Enable microphone access in privacy settings.

Step 3: Update Drivers

Update your audio drivers from Device Manager.

Step 4: Restart Your PC

Sometimes a simple restart fixes the issue.

Conclusion: These steps should fix your microphone problem easily.
